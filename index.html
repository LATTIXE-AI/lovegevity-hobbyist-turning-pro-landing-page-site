Do I need prior experience or education to enroll?+
Not at all. Our program is designed for people exactly like you — someone with natural talent, passion, and real-world experience who simply hasn't had formal training. If you've helped plan even one event, you already have what it takes to get started.
How long does it take to complete the certification?+
Most students complete the core certification in 4–8 weeks, studying 5–7 hours per week. It's fully self-paced, so you can go faster or slower depending on your life. The business tools — templates, scripts, pricing — are available the moment you enroll.

Absolutely. Many of our students already have clients but are stuck on pricing, proposals, or growing beyond friends and family. The program helps you raise your rates, win preferred-vendor status, and build a referral engine that doesn't depend on your personal network.
